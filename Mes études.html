BUT RT

THE NETWORKS & TELECOMS B.U.T is a National Diploma at BAC +3 level offered and issued by the IUTs. It trains the digital technology specialists of tomorrow. It is a professional training recognized and appreciated by companies allowing rapid professional integration in a booming business sector. Continuation of studies in Engineering Schools or Masters is possible at the end of the second year or after obtaining the BUT. The main areas studied are: Computer networks (administration and security of systems and infrastructures) Web and Internet technologies Wireless and wired communications Computer programming Transversal tools (Mathematics, Electronics, Expression/Communication, Professional English, IT and Internet Law)

After spending 3 months in RT here is my feeling. I'm rather surprised at the speed at which we learn, I think I learned more in 3 months of RT than in all of my 2 years in STI2D. I also notice that I have a considerable delay compared to the rest of the class especially in English in maths but also in network administration. However, I like what we are learning and I think I will invest a lot more to be able to continue. I plan to continue with the ROM or Cyber course next year on a work-study basis. I can't wait to discover the world of work.
